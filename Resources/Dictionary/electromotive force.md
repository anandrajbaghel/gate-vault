### Electromotive Force (EMF)
*Electrical Circuits & Machines*

It refers to the voltage generated by a source (like a battery or an alternator) to drive current in a circuit.
==or==
The energy per unit charge supplied by a source, driving current flow in a circuit (measured in Volts).

> See [[Magnetomotive Force|Magnetomotive Force (MMF)]]
