### Operator
*Mathematics : Transformations on Functions/Vectors*

A rule or mapping that takes one function/vector and produces another, often representing differentiation, integration, or linear transformations.

---
**Examples:**

- **Differential Operator:** $\frac{d}{dx} f(x)$
- **Laplacian Operator:** $\nabla^2 f$
- **Integral Operator:** $\displaystyle \int_0^x f(t),dt$
- **Linear Operator:** $T(\mathbf{x}) = A\mathbf{x}$ where ($A$) is a matrix
- **Shift Operator (Signals):** $T{x[n]} = x[n-1]$
