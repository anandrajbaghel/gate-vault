### Conjugate Signal
*Signals & Systems / Complex Analysis*

The signal obtained by taking the complex conjugate of a complex-valued signal, reversing the sign of the imaginary part.
##### Mathematically

If $x(t) = a(t) + j b(t)$, then $$x^*(t) = a(t) - j b(t)$$

---
##### Use in EE

Important in energy calculations, correlation, Fourier analysis, and power computation of complex signals.
