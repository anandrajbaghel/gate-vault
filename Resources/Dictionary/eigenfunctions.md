### Eigenfunctions
*Operators & Signal/Systems Analysis*

A function that, when an [[operator]] (such as a derivative, integral, or system operator) acts on it, gives back the **same function**, only multiplied by a constant called the **[[Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors|eigenvalues]]**.

---
**Examples:**

- For the differential operator $\frac{d}{dx}$, the function $e^{kx}$ is an eigenfunction.
- For the Laplacian $\nabla^2$, sinusoidal functions like $\sin(kx)$ are eigenfunctions.
- In LTI systems, complex exponentials $e^{st}$ are eigenfunctions of the system.
