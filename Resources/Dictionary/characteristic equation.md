### Characteristic Equation
*Control Systems & Circuit Analysis*

An algebraic equation obtained from a system’s differential or difference equation, used to determine system stability and [[natural response]].
$$T(s) = \frac{G(s)H(s)}{1+G(s)H(s)}$$
Characteristic equation of $T(s)$ :

$$\boxed{\quad 1+G(s)H(s)=0\quad}$$
