### Electromagnetic Force
*Electromagnetism & Electrical Machines*

**Lorentz Force** $$\mathbf{F} = q(\mathbf{E} + \mathbf{v} \times \mathbf{B})$$
Force experienced by a current-carrying conductor in a magnetic field, given by

**For a current-carrying conductor** $$F = BIL \sin{\theta}$$

---
Used in motors, solenoids, relays, and electromechanical systems.
